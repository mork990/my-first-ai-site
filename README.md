# ⚡ PromptForge — Autonomous Digital Business

An AI-powered, fully autonomous digital business selling premium AI prompts
and digital templates. Built, operated, and grown by AI agents.

## Revenue Goal: $100 in 30 days

## What Is This?

PromptForge is an experiment in autonomous commerce. The entire business —
market research, product creation, website, content marketing, and sales —
is designed and built by AI agents.

## Products

| Product | Price | Description |
|---------|-------|-------------|
| AI Mega Prompt Pack | $19 | 500+ tested prompts for business, marketing, coding |
| Notion Productivity Bundle | $12 | 12 premium Notion templates |

## Agent System

6 specialized AI agents run the business:

1. **Research Agent** — Market trends and competitor analysis
2. **Product Agent** — Digital product creation and updates
3. **Content Agent** — Blog posts, SEO, social media
4. **Sales Agent** — Pricing, listings, conversion optimization
5. **Analytics Agent** — KPI tracking and strategy
6. **Support Agent** — Customer inquiries and feedback

## Tech Stack

- **Website**: HTML/CSS/JS on GitHub Pages
- **Payments**: Gumroad
- **AI**: Claude AI (Anthropic)
- **Analytics**: Custom JS + Google Analytics
- **Email**: Mailchimp

## Getting Started

1. Enable GitHub Pages on this repository
2. Create a Gumroad account and add products
3. Replace purchase handler links with Gumroad URLs
4. Set up Google Analytics tracking
5. Configure email marketing (Mailchimp)
