# Autonomous Digital Business Plan - PromptForge

## Executive Summary
**PromptForge** is a fully autonomous digital business selling AI prompt packs,
templates, and digital productivity tools. The business operates with minimal
human intervention using automated agent workflows for market research, content
creation, SEO, sales, and customer support.

## Revenue Goal: $100 in 30 Days

### Product Line & Pricing Strategy

| Product | Price | Monthly Target Sales | Revenue |
|---------|-------|---------------------|---------|
| AI Mega Prompt Pack (500+ prompts) | $19 | 3 | $57 |
| Notion Productivity Bundle | $12 | 2 | $24 |
| Social Media Content Calendar | $9 | 3 | $27 |
| **Total** | | **8 sales** | **$108** |

### Target Market
- **Primary**: Solopreneurs, freelancers, and small business owners (25-45)
- **Secondary**: Content creators, marketers, and startup founders
- **Geography**: Global (English-speaking markets)
- **Pain Points**: Time-consuming AI prompting, disorganized workflows, content planning

### Market Research Findings
1. **AI Tools Market**: Growing 25%+ YoY, expected $300B+ by 2027
2. **Digital Templates**: Etsy/Gumroad template sales up 180% since 2024
3. **Prompt Engineering**: Search interest up 400% in 24 months
4. **Key Competitors**: PromptBase, AIPRM, various Gumroad sellers
5. **Opportunity**: Bundled, niche-specific prompt packs with templates

### Distribution Channels
1. **Gumroad** - Primary sales platform (0% fee on free plan + payment processing)
2. **GitHub Pages** - Main website and content hub (free hosting)
3. **Social Media** - Twitter/X, LinkedIn, Reddit for organic reach
4. **SEO/Blog** - Long-tail keyword content for organic traffic
