The 25 Best AI Prompts for Business in 2026

March 2, 2026 · 8 min read

We tested over 1,000 prompts across ChatGPT, Claude, and Gemini to find the absolute best ones for business use. Here are the top 25 that consistently deliver outstanding results.

Each prompt is ready to copy-paste — just replace the [BRACKETS] with your specifics.

Strategy & Planning

1. Business Model Analysis

Analyze my business model for [BUSINESS TYPE]. Identify the top 3 revenue opportunities I'm missing, potential risks, and specific actions I should take this quarter to increase revenue by 20%. Be specific and actionable.

2. Competitive Intelligence

Act as a competitive intelligence analyst. Compare [MY COMPANY] with [COMPETITOR 1] and [COMPETITOR 2]. Create a detailed matrix covering: pricing, features, target market, strengths, weaknesses, and untapped opportunities. Format as a table.

3. Quarterly OKR Generator

You are a strategic planning expert. Based on these company goals: [GOALS], create a set of OKRs (Objectives and Key Results) for Q[QUARTER] [YEAR]. Each objective should have 3-4 measurable key results with specific numbers.

Marketing & Copywriting

4. Landing Page Copy

Write high-converting landing page copy for [PRODUCT/SERVICE]. Target audience: [AUDIENCE]. Key benefit: [BENEFIT]. Include: headline, subheadline, 3 feature sections with benefits, 2 testimonial placeholders, and a CTA. Use the PAS (Problem-Agitate-Solve) framework.

5. Email Sequence Builder

Create a 5-email welcome sequence for new subscribers to [BUSINESS TYPE]. Goal: build trust and convert to [PRODUCT] purchase. Each email should include: subject line (with open rate optimization), preview text, body copy, and CTA. Tone: [TONE].

6. Social Media Content Batch

Generate 20 social media posts for [PLATFORM] about [TOPIC]. Mix of: educational (40%), engaging questions (20%), promotional (20%), behind-the-scenes (20%). Include relevant hashtags. Target audience: [AUDIENCE]. Brand voice: [VOICE].

7. Ad Copy Variations

Write 10 Facebook/Google ad variations for [PRODUCT]. Each ad should have: headline (max 30 chars), description (max 90 chars), and CTA. Test different angles: pain point, benefit, social proof, urgency, curiosity. Target: [AUDIENCE].

Sales & Outreach

8. Cold Email Template

Write a cold outreach email to [ROLE] at [COMPANY TYPE]. My product: [PRODUCT] solves [PROBLEM]. Use the AIDA framework. Keep it under 150 words. Include a specific, low-commitment CTA. Avoid spam trigger words.

9. Objection Handler

List the top 10 sales objections for [PRODUCT/SERVICE] priced at [PRICE]. For each objection, provide: the objection, why customers say it, a 2-3 sentence response that addresses the concern, and a follow-up question to keep the conversation going.

10. Proposal Generator

Create a professional project proposal for [SERVICE] for a client in [INDUSTRY]. Include: executive summary, scope of work, deliverables, timeline (4 weeks), pricing ($[AMOUNT]), terms, and next steps. Tone: professional but approachable.

Content Creation

11. Blog Post Outline

Create a detailed blog post outline for "[TITLE]". Target keyword: [KEYWORD]. Include: H2 and H3 headers, key points under each, word count targets per section, internal link opportunities, and a meta description. Optimize for featured snippets.

12. Video Script

Write a [LENGTH]-minute YouTube script about [TOPIC]. Structure: hook (first 15 seconds), problem statement, 3 main points with examples, CTA. Target audience: [AUDIENCE]. Style: [casual/professional/educational]. Include B-roll suggestions.

13. Newsletter Template

Write a weekly newsletter edition about [TOPIC] for [AUDIENCE]. Format: catchy subject line, personal intro (2 sentences), 3 curated insights with my commentary, 1 actionable tip, and a soft product mention for [PRODUCT]. Keep it under 500 words.

Productivity & Operations

14. Meeting Summary & Actions

From these meeting notes: [PASTE NOTES]. Create: 1) A concise summary (3-5 bullets), 2) Action items with owners and deadlines, 3) Decisions made, 4) Open questions to follow up on. Format clearly with headers.

15. SOP Creator

Create a Standard Operating Procedure (SOP) for [PROCESS]. Include: purpose, scope, step-by-step instructions (numbered), required tools, common mistakes to avoid, quality checklist, and estimated time per step. Format for easy reference.

16. Hiring Evaluator

Create an evaluation framework for hiring a [ROLE]. Include: 10 interview questions (mix of technical and behavioral), ideal answer benchmarks, scoring rubric (1-5), red flags to watch for, and a final decision matrix. Based on these requirements: [REQUIREMENTS].

Data & Analysis

17. Data Interpreter

Analyze this data: [PASTE DATA]. Identify: top 3 trends, anomalies, correlations, and actionable insights. Present findings as: executive summary (2 sentences), key findings (bullets), recommended actions, and what additional data would be helpful.

18. Customer Feedback Analyzer

Analyze these customer reviews/feedback: [PASTE FEEDBACK]. Categorize by: sentiment (positive/negative/neutral), topic, frequency. Identify the top 5 themes, urgent issues, and 3 quick wins to improve customer satisfaction.

Creative & Brainstorming

19. Product Name Generator

Generate 30 product name ideas for [DESCRIPTION]. Requirements: easy to pronounce, memorable, available as .com domain (suggest variations), conveys [FEELING/VALUE]. Organize by style: modern, playful, professional, abstract.

20. Problem-Solution Brainstorm

I'm facing this challenge: [CHALLENGE]. Act as a panel of 3 experts: a strategist, an engineer, and a customer advocate. Each expert should propose 3 different solutions with pros/cons. Then synthesize the best approach.

Advanced Prompts

21. SWOT Analysis Deep Dive

Perform a comprehensive SWOT analysis for [BUSINESS] in the [INDUSTRY] market. For each quadrant, provide 5+ points with specific examples. Then create a strategy matrix: SO (use strengths to capture opportunities), WO, ST, and WT strategies.

22. Financial Model Helper

Help me build a financial model for [BUSINESS TYPE]. Revenue assumptions: [DETAILS]. Create a 12-month projection including: MRR, churn, CAC, LTV, burn rate, and breakeven point. Include formulas I can use in a spreadsheet.

23. Crisis Communication

Draft a crisis communication plan for [SCENARIO] at [COMPANY]. Include: initial response (within 1 hour), public statement, internal memo, social media response template, FAQ for customer support, and follow-up communication timeline.

24. Pitch Deck Outline

Create a 12-slide pitch deck outline for [STARTUP/PRODUCT]. Include slide-by-slide content: problem, solution, market size (TAM/SAM/SOM), business model, traction, competition, team, financials, ask. Add speaker notes for each slide.

25. Meta-Prompt: Prompt Optimizer

I want to achieve [GOAL] using AI. My current prompt is: "[YOUR PROMPT]". Analyze my prompt and improve it by: adding specificity, including output format, adding constraints, including examples, and optimizing for the best possible response. Explain each improvement you made.
