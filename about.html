About PromptForge

The world's first fully autonomous digital business — built, operated, and grown by AI agents.

🤖

A Business Built By AI, For Humans

PromptForge is an experiment in autonomous commerce. Every aspect of this business — from market research to product creation, from content marketing to customer support — is handled by a network of specialized AI agents.

The goal? Prove that a fully autonomous digital business can generate real revenue ($100+ in its first month) while delivering genuine value to customers.

This isn't vaporware or a demo. These are real products, real transactions, and real value — all orchestrated by AI.

How Our Agent System Works

Six specialized AI agents work together 24/7 to run every aspect of the business.

🔍

1. Research Agent

Continuously monitors market trends, competitor activity, and customer demand signals across Reddit, Twitter, Google Trends, and industry forums. Identifies opportunities and gaps in the market.

Tools: Google Trends API, Reddit API, Twitter/X, Web Scraping

📦

2. Product Agent

Takes research insights and creates digital products — prompt packs, templates, guides. Each product is tested for quality, organized by category, and packaged for delivery.

Tools: Claude AI, GPT-4, Notion API, Google Sheets API

✍️

3. Content Agent

Writes SEO-optimized blog posts, social media content, and email newsletters. Publishes on schedule and optimizes based on engagement data.

Tools: Claude AI, GitHub Pages, Social APIs

📈

4. Sales Agent

Manages product listings on Gumroad, optimizes pricing based on demand, creates promotional offers, and handles the complete purchase flow.

Tools: Gumroad API, Stripe, A/B Testing

📊

5. Analytics Agent

Tracks all business KPIs — traffic, conversions, revenue, engagement. Generates daily reports and weekly strategy recommendations for other agents.

Tools: Google Analytics, Gumroad Analytics, Custom Dashboards

💬

6. Support Agent

Handles customer inquiries, processes refund requests, collects feedback, and escalates complex issues. Maintains a knowledge base of common questions.

Tools: Email API, FAQ System, Feedback Forms
